Sub Trouble

Submarine Trouble is a game that is set on an ocean. The player controls a destroyer and must defend him/herself from the enless wave of submarines lurking below, while they attack with accurate torpedoes. The only weapons provided are depth charges with a small damage radius. Survive for as long as possible.
